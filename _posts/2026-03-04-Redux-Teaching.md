Redux Teaching is a passion project of mine. All of the knowledge I have gathered throughout my career I would like to give back to the community, hence the creation of Redux Teaching. The focus of the channel is to guide other educators or trainers through the process of developing curriculum or training using the Dick and Carey model of instructional design. It all begins with the identification of goals, to the iterative improvement of instruction based on self applied field observations.
